Animal Growth, Outreach and Care (AGCO)

Start Date: 2024-02-21

End Date: 2024-08-31

Project ID: 2431

Project Lead: John Cater

Growth

  • Implement a nutrition assessment program for all animals to ensure dietary needs are met.
  • Develop and introduce an enrichment schedule to enhance mental stimulation and physical activity for animals.
  • Conduct regular health checks for all animals to identify and address any medical concerns promptly.

Outreach

  • Organize and execute at least two educational workshops per month to educate the community on responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.
  • Establish partnerships with local schools or youth organizations to facilitate youth engagement and education initiatives.
  • Launch a social media campaign highlighting adoptable animals and success stories to increase public awareness and engagement.

Care

  • Develop a comprehensive training program for volunteers on animal handling and care protocols.
  • Implement a system for regular grooming sessions for all animals to promote hygiene and well-being.
  • Establish a protocol for behavioral assessments and training plans for animals with specific needs to improve adoptability and quality of life.
